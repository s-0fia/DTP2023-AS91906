What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. They contain information that is transferred to your device's memory and help provide a more personalized experience. Cookies may also collect information about your usage patterns and preferences.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies in the the Website:

a. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the functioning of the the Website and enable core features such as user authentication and security. They allow you to navigate the app, access secure areas, and use its features. These cookies do not gather any personal information and are automatically expire after 6 hours after authentication.

b. Personalization Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide a more personalized experience. They store information such as your name, language, etc. These cookies help enhance your user experience but are not used to track your browsing activity outside of the the Website.

Information Stored in Cookies

The cookies used in the the Website store the following information:

a. Email: We store your email address in cookies to facilitate the login process and verify your identity.

b. Name: Your name is stored in cookies to personalize your experience within the app and enhance user engagement.

c. User ID: We assign a unique user ID to each user, which is stored in cookies for identification and authentication purposes.

d. Profile Picture URL: If you choose to upload a profile picture (or authenticate via a google account), the URL of your picture may be stored in cookies to display it within the app.
